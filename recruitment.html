Interest Form

We welcome ALL who wish to serve to join our organization! We DO NOT haze. We DO NOT interview. In order to successfully rush, you must submit a Letter of Intent AND attend:

  • 1 Information Session
  • 1 Leadership Workshop (L)
  • 1 Fellowship Event (F)
  • 2 Service Events (S)

If you have any questions, email us at pledgemaster@apogamma.org or pledgeteam@apogamma.org.
